Volumetric Smoke Simulation

Smoke is a fascinating example of fluid dynamics in action. It combines:

  • Advection: Smoke particles move with the velocity field
  • Diffusion: Smoke spreads out and dissipates over time
  • Buoyancy: Hot smoke rises due to temperature differences
  • Vorticity confinement: Preserves swirling details
